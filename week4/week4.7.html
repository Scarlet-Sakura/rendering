Question :

The radiance L = 6000 * cos(θ) W/(m^2 sr) for a non - diffuse emitter of area 10 by 10 cm. Find the radiant exitance. Also, find the power of the entire light source.

Answer :

first intergal is over 0 -> 2pi and second over 0 -> pi/2

M = ∬ L * cos(θ)* sin(θ) dθda

this intergal is over 0 -> pi/2

= 2 * pi * ∫ * L * cos(θ) * sin(θ) dθ

= 2 * pi * ∫ * 6000 * cos^2(Θ) * sin(θ) dθ

= 2 * pi * 2000 = 12567 W/m^2

power of the light source : M * A = 12567 * 0.01 = 125.67 Watt.
